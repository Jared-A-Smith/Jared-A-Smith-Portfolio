What's Left?

The small community of Tomball located northwest of Houston Texas, has long been considered a "sleepy old town". As a native Houstonian, I recall trips to my grandmotherâ€™s house in Tomball once feeling as though we were traveling to "no-where land". However, in recent years this once sleepy town has waken and is experiencing exponential growth and development. In 2022, City Administration tasked me with creating a map that could help answer the question "What's Left?" to be developed in this small community. Using the most recent aerial imagery available coupled with permitting data I've created the "What's Left?" map. This map is a graduated symbol map that identifies the location, approximate size and current zoning of vacant undeveloped parcels in Tomball, TX. ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator were utilized in creating this map.
