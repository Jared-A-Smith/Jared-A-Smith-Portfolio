About Me

My name is Jared Smith, I’m currently serving as the City Planner in Tomball, TX. I graduated from Texas State Univeristy with a Bachelor of Science in Geography and I’m a graduate student at the University of Wisconsin pursuing a Masters of Science in Cartography and GIS Development. Prior to my current role as City Planner for Tomball, I was the Planning & Development Services Manager for Abilene, TX. While serving these municaplities I’ve focused on creating and implementing GIS solutions to effeciently manage a wide range of city projects and datasets. Thanks for visiting!
